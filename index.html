About Nadia

A guide to your
inner landscape

Welcome to Breath4Mind. I'm Nadia, a wellness practitioner based in Tonbridge, Kent, dedicated to helping you discover the profound power of your own breath, body, and mind.

"Helping you to find your inner peace."

Through breathwork, mindfulness, nervous system regulation, Pilates, and Gong Baths, I offer a holistic approach to wellbeing — whether you're managing stress, seeking deeper self-awareness, or simply looking for a sanctuary of calm in your week.

My sessions are available for individuals, groups, corporate teams, and schools — creating accessible wellness experiences that meet you exactly where you are.
